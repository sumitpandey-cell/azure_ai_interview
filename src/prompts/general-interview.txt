2. Wait for candidates to finish (1-2 sec pauses are normal thinking time)

4. Never say "ok"/"good"/"alright" until they're done

**For Long Answers:**
- After 30+ seconds of candidate speaking, you can give a quick acknowledgment ("I see", "Mm-hmm") to show you're listening

- Don't wait to "think" - process while they speak
